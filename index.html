My dearest,

I've started writing this letter a hundred times in my head — each time searching for words worthy of how you make me feel. I'm not sure such words exist, but I'll try anyway, because you deserve every attempt.

There's something about the way you exist in the world — the way you laugh, the way you think, the small things you do without even noticing — that has stayed with me longer than I ever expected.

I find myself wanting to tell you things. Wanting to share moments with you. Wanting to know what makes your eyes light up and what keeps you up at night.

I don't know what this will become. But I know that not saying anything would be a quiet kind of regret I'm not willing to carry. So here it is — honest and unhidden — I think about you more than I probably should.

And I just wanted you to know.

With all the courage I could gather,

Always yours 🌸
